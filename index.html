Hongyi James Cai

Duke University hongyi.cai@duke.edu

I am a fourth-year undergraduate student at Duke University, majoring in Computer Science and Statistics. I currently work with Shuyan Zhou and Bhuwan Dhingra at DukeNLP. I am also lucky to be mentored by many great PhDs at Duke, including Junlin Wang, Sanxing Chen, and Xunjian Yin.

My research interests lie in language model reasoning, multi agent system, and continual learning.
